Beginner Guidance

What is P2P Trading?
P2P (Peer-to-Peer) trading lets you buy and sell crypto directly with other users at agreed prices, with Bitegit acting as an escrow to keep your funds safe.
How to Buy USDT on P2P?
1. Select "Buy" tab and choose USDT.
2. Pick an offer that suits your limit & payment method.
3. Enter amount and click Buy.
4. Pay the seller via the selected payment method.
5. Confirm payment — USDT will be released to your wallet.
How to Sell USDT on P2P?
1. Select "Sell" tab and choose USDT.
2. Pick a buy offer from a merchant.
3. Enter amount and confirm.
4. Wait for the buyer to send payment.
5. Confirm receipt — USDT will be released from escrow.
KYC & Security Settings
Complete KYC verification to unlock higher trading limits. Go to Profile → KYC Verification and submit your Aadhaar front, back, and selfie. Verification takes 24–48 hours.
Deposit & Withdrawal
Deposit crypto from any wallet to your Bitegit address. Withdrawals are processed after identity verification. Minimum withdrawal limits apply per asset.
